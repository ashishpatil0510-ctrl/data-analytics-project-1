Insights & Recommendations
📈
Q4 2011 — Peak Season Surge
Revenue hit £1.51M in November alone — the single highest month. Q4 2011 was 30% above Q3. This is a clear gifting/holiday pattern. Recommendation: Pre-load inventory and launch marketing campaigns by September each year to capture this surge early.
🏆
Star Products Drive Disproportionate Revenue
The top 10 products contribute a significant share of total revenue. Regency Cakestand 3 Tier (£174K) and Paper Craft Little Birdie (£168K) are standouts. Recommendation: Protect stock levels on these SKUs and explore bundle offers to increase basket size.
🌍
UK Dependency — Diversification Needed
The United Kingdom accounts for 85% of total revenue. Netherlands (£285K) and EIRE (£283K) are the only international markets crossing £250K. Recommendation: Invest in targeted campaigns for Germany and France which show high order frequency but low average value.
💡
Average Order Value Opportunity
Average order value is £534. High-volume, low-margin products (e.g. Cake Cases, Retrospot Bags) inflate unit counts but suppress revenue per order. Recommendation: Bundle low-margin bestsellers with premium items. Introduce a "Free shipping above £600" incentive to nudge AOV upward.
